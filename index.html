About Test 1 Bakery

Founded with the vision of bringing authentic artisan baking to Singapore's heart, Test 1 Bakery is your oasis for handcrafted breads and pastries made the traditional way.

We take pride in using natural fermentation techniques and seasonal ingredients sourced from trusted local partners. Our bakery is a warm, welcoming space designed especially for working professionals seeking quality and comfort in every bite.
